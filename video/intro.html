MuDiPA
A Human-in-the-Loop AI-Assisted Platform for
Discourse-Graph Annotation in Multi-Party Dialogues
Annotating SDRT discourse structure in multi-party dialogue is slow and inconsistent. MuDiPA keeps the human in control and pairs their judgment with AI assistance — suggestions, rationales, and structure — to make the task faster and more reliable.

Suggestion engine

Proposes the most likely attachment and relation label, each with a confidence score.

Reasoning engine

Vets a chosen relation against its definition — holds or doesn’t — with a rationale.

Sub-dialogue navigation

Groups the dialogue into coherent conversational threads, each explainable on demand.

Multimodal & extensible

Synchronized video, audio and text; multiple corpora and pluggable back-ends.
